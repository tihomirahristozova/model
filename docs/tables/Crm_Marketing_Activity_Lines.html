    Table Crm_Marketing_Activity_Lines

    Entity

    Entity: Crm.Marketing.MarketingActivityLines

    Detail records (lines) of the marketing activities. Entity: Crm_Marketing_Activity_Lines (Introduced in version 22.1.5.56)

    Owner Tables Hierarchy

    • Crm_Activities
    • Cm_Activities
    • Gen_Documents

    Summary

    Name Type Description
    Competitor_Id uniqueidentifier Competitor where marketing activity was held.
    Competitor_Price decimal(15, 5) Price of the competitor
    In_Store_Available_Quantity decimal(10, 3) The quantity found in store (in Quantity Unit). NULL means that count was not performed.
    In_Store_Location nvarchar(32) Location in store, like row, stand, etc.
    In_Store_Price decimal(15, 5) Price displayed in store.
    Line_No int Consecutive line number, unique within the marketing activity. Usually is increasing in steps of 10, like in 10, 20, 30, etc.
    Marketing_Activity_Id uniqueidentifier Marketing activity
    Marketing_Activity_Line_Id uniqueidentifier PK
    Marketing_Activity_Line_Type_Id uniqueidentifier Describes what the purpose of the marketing activity is.
    Notes nvarchar(max)
    Order_Quantity decimal(15, 3) Recommended quantity (in Quantity Unit) for new order as a result of the marketing activity. NULL means that there is no specific recommendation.
    Order_Quantity_Unit_Id uniqueidentifier The measurement unit of In_Store_Available_Quantity and Order_Quantity.
    Product_Id uniqueidentifier The specific product that was target of the marketing activity.
    Row_Version timestamp
