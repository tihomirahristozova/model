    Table Crm_Leads

    Entity

    Entity: Crm.Presales.Leads

    Leads are potential customers for your products. Because leads are usually processed in a hurry or imported from external lists, personal and company information is saved in a less structured way. With the advancement of the qualification process (including deduplication), the information is structured in the usual Person and Company entities. When successfully processed, leads can be converted to Opportunities. Entity: Crm_Leads (Introduced in version 21.1.3.78)

    Summary

    Name Type Description
    Assigned_To_Sales_Person_Id uniqueidentifier Sales rep to whom this lead is assigned for further processing. NULL - not yet assigned.
    Campaign_Id uniqueidentifier The marketing campaign under which the lead was generated.
    Company_Name nvarchar(64) The name of the company, which the person works for (in relation to the current lead).
    Converted_To_Deal_Id uniqueidentifier Filled when the lead is converted to opportunity (deal).
    Creation_Timestamp_Utc datetime Readonly System assigned date and time (in UTC), when the lead was initially created.
    Enterprise_Company_Id uniqueidentifier The enterprise company to which this lead belongs.
    Enterprise_Company_Location_Id uniqueidentifier The enterprise company location to which this lead belongs.
    Identified_Company_Id uniqueidentifier Filled when the company is identified within our database.
    Identified_Person_Id uniqueidentifier Filled when the person is identified and recorded in our master records.
    Lead_Id uniqueidentifier PK
    Lead_User_Stage_Id uniqueidentifier The user-defined sub-stage of the lead. This also defines the step in the sales sequence and the next appropriate activity to engage the lead. NULL when the user stage is undefined (the pure System Stage defines the stage).
    Marketing_Company_Size_Id uniqueidentifier The size class of the company.
    Marketing_Industry_Id uniqueidentifier The industry (for marketing purposes) of the lead.
    Marketing_Solution_Id uniqueidentifier The marketing solution, in which the lead is interested or which generated the lead.
    MQL_Timestamp_Utc datetime Readonly System assigned date and time (in UTC), when the lead was first promoted to Marketing Qualified Lead (MQL).
    Owner_User_Id uniqueidentifier The user, responsible for this record.
    Person_Email nvarchar(64) The email address of the sales lead person.
    Person_First_Name nvarchar(64) The first name of the person, representing the sales lead.
    Person_Last_Name nvarchar(64) The last name of the person, representing the sales lead.
    Person_Mobile_Phone nvarchar(32) The mobile phone of the sales lead person.
    Person_Salutation nvarchar(16) Salutation for the sales lead person.
    Person_Web_Profile nvarchar(64) Web address (including protocol like https) of the personal profile in a social or professional network. The address itself, as it is full valid Internet address, contains also the network name.
    Person_Work_Phone nvarchar(32) The work phone of the sales lead person.
    Role_In_Company nvarchar(32) The role of the person in the company.
    Row_Version timestamp
    Sales_Area_Id uniqueidentifier The sales area, where the lead is located, when it is identified. This should also indicate the Time Zone.
    SQL_Timestamp_Utc datetime Readonly System assigned date and time (in UTC), when the lead was first promoted to Sales Qualified Lead.
    System_Stage nvarchar(3) Allowed: NEW, QUA, MQL, SQL, CLO The system stage of the lead - New, Qualifying, Marketing Qualified Lead, Sales Qualified Lead, Closed. (NEW, QUA, MQL, SQL, CLO)
